Zany Zombies

A funny physics-based shooting game. Try your skills in the incredible battles against crafty zombies!

The Idea

Nasty zombies are trying to disturb the peace of the living. Beat them back with your magic slingshot and powerful ammo and a little help from friendly ghosts as well.

The Features

  • FREE TO PLAY!
  • Physics based zaniness
  • Valuable boosts and magic weapons available at every level
  • Endless Survival mode
  • Cool accelerometer based mechanics: use tilting and pulling to shoot magic weapons
  • Gamecenter leaderboard
  • Beat your Facebook friends’ scores!
  • Survive all 25 levels with three stars (if you can)!
